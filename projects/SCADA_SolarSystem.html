SCADA Solar System

During my internship, I worked on a project called SCADA solar system. It involved creating an interface in Mango automation that allows for monitoring of a solar system across multiple sites.

Features

  1. Monitoring multiple sites.
  2. Flow charts
  3. Solar PV monitoring: Power, Temperature, Solar Radiation, Status.
  4. Battery monitoring: Power, SoC, Current, status.
  5. Costumer monitoring: Voltage, Current, Power, Energy
